To delete you Flety provider account:

1. Open the Flety Provider app.

2. Tap on Profile tab and click on "Delete Account" button. (OR)

3. Please reach out to hola@flety.io with your name, email ID, phone number and they will contact you regarding the deletion of your account.

Deleting your account will:

1. Remove your profile and data from Flety services.

2. Deactivate your account immediately with permanent deletion.

3. Retain certain information as required by law.
